How to Use

ArtiScapes is optimized for landscape orientation.

Your device is currently in portrait orientation.

  • To generate a new random landscape picture, click or tap the Next Image button.
  • To use the timer option, input the minutes into the timer display and click or tap the Set Timer button. When the timer reaches 00:00, a new image will be generated.
  • To hide the user interface (UI), click the Hide UI button in the lower left corner. Click again to bring the UI back.
  • To see up to last six images generated, click or tap the Image History button.
  • To find near by scenic places, click on Dropdown button Where to Paint and to hide the place click on Dropup arrow button.
